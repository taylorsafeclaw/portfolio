Focus Cards Aceternity Focus Cards repurposed: hovering one card makes non-hovered cards descend the density ramp — characters in their borders dim, text drops to --fg-quietest. Not gaussian blur — density-based dimming. The focused card's border characters climb the ramp. new
Card Spotlight Aceternity Card Spotlight repurposed: cursor creates a density gradient on the card surface. Instead of a light radial gradient, characters in the card's background climb the ramp near the cursor. A mini halo inside each card. Reveals subtle detail text as cursor moves across. new
Direction Hover Aceternity Direction Aware repurposed: when you approach a card, ASCII characters ripple inward from the direction of approach. Enter from the left → characters cascade left-to-right. Enter from below → characters cascade bottom-to-top. The card acknowledges where you came from. new
Text Reveal Aceternity Text Reveal Card repurposed: moving cursor across a card progressively reveals a hidden detail line. The reveal uses density erosion — characters part like curtains as your cursor sweeps across, exposing the detail text beneath. new
Moving Border Aceternity Moving Border repurposed: a density character (▓) travels the perimeter of the focused card — a slow orbit that marks it as active. Not a glowing pixel — an ASCII character walking the border. 3-4 second loop. new
Header Decode MagicUI HyperText repurposed: "selected work" header scrambles through density ramp characters on scroll-in, then resolves. Uses existing ScrambleLink logic extended to viewport trigger. extend

Synergy: Focus + Spotlight + Direction + Text Reveal + Moving Border all work together on each card. Approach → direction ripple. Hover → focus dims others + spotlight activates + moving border starts + hidden text begins revealing. Leave → everything settles. Five interactions, one seamless moment.
