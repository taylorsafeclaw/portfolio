Story — who I am

Density Erosion Aceternity Canvas Reveal inverted — ASCII erodes outward from center (█→▓→▒→░→gone), clearing space for text. Characters linger at margins, breathing. new
Text Generate Aceternity Text Generate — words crystallize one by one. Each word flashes through density ramp (░→▒→letter) before resolving. Paced to reading speed. new
Split Text Entry ReactBits SplitText — per-character staggered animation for the key sentence. Each character animates independently with slight delay. Creates a wave of text materializing. new
Lamp Gradient Aceternity Lamp — density gradient radiates from text center. Denser chars near the narrative, fading outward. Visual weight without color. new

Synergy: Erosion clears space → Lamp creates visual gravity at center → Text Generate fills the cleared space word by word → Split Text adds per-character life to the key phrase. Four effects, one seamless moment: the field parts, the words arrive.

Work — what I've built

Header Decode MagicUI HyperText — "selected work" scrambles through density ramp chars on scroll-in, resolves. Extends existing ScrambleLink to viewport trigger. extend
Focus Cards Aceternity Focus Cards — hovering one card dims others via density descent. Non-focused cards' text drops to --fg-quietest, border chars descend ramp. Focused card brightens. new
Card Spotlight Aceternity Card Spotlight — cursor creates density gradient on card surface. Mini-halo inside each card. Background chars climb ramp near cursor, revealing subtle texture. new
Direction Hover Aceternity Direction Aware — ASCII chars ripple inward from approach direction. Enter from left → cascade left-to-right. Card acknowledges where you came from. new
Text Reveal Aceternity Text Reveal Card — sweeping cursor across card reveals hidden detail text. Density chars part like curtains as cursor moves, exposing deeper info beneath. new
Moving Border Aceternity Moving Border — density char (▓) orbits the focused card perimeter. 3-4s loop. Not a glow — an ASCII character walking the border. new
Shimmer Border hover.dev Shimmer Border — subtle shimmer travels the card edge. Repurposed: the shimmer is a brightness wave through border characters, not a light effect. Complements moving border on different cards. new
Hover Image Links hover.dev Hover Image Links — hovering a project title reveals a preview image/visual that follows cursor. Repurposed: instead of a photo, an ASCII art rendering of the project appears near cursor (uses Aceternity ASCII Art component or pre-rendered ASCII). For v1 with placeholders, shows abstract density patterns per project. new

Synergy: Approach card → direction ripple. Hover → focus dims others + spotlight activates + moving border starts orbiting + shimmer pulses. Sweep cursor → text reveal parts the curtain. All powered by the density ramp. The card is having a conversation with you.

Conviction — what I believe

Scroll Pacing Each statement on its own scroll beat. 80-100px between them. One thought at a time. The page slows down. css
Emphasis Decode Key phrases appear through slow density decode (~600ms vs headers' 300ms). Characters climb the ramp deliberately. The emphasis earns its brightness. extend
Field Resonance Breathing tempo shifts per statement — faster pulse for intense lines, slower for reflective ones. The field responds to emotional weight. new
Evervault Texture Aceternity Evervault — background has subtly cycling density chars at very low opacity. Like the page is thinking. Cursor hovering near text calms the texture — chars slow and settle. new
Vanish Text hover.dev Vanish Text repurposed — as you scroll past a conviction statement, it doesn't just leave the viewport. Characters dissolve back into the density ramp (letter→█→▓→▒→░→·). The thought returns to the field. Complement to Text Generate. new

Synergy: Erosion opens → statement decodes in slowly → field resonance adjusts breathing → Evervault texture thinks around the words → scroll away → Vanish dissolves it back to the field. Content emerges from and returns to the ASCII substrate. The field is always the ground truth.

Connective Tissue — between sections
