The full page as one living system

Five sections, each with its own rhythm. The ASCII field is the connective tissue — everything emerges from it and returns to it.

The unifying idea

The entire page is an ASCII field. Content doesn't sit on top of it — content emerges from it. As you scroll, the field erodes to reveal each section. When you scroll away, it reclaims the space. Your cursor always affects the field. The page breathes. Every section is a clearing in the noise where meaning has crystallized.

The effects system — how they connect

PERSISTENT (always active)
Breathing field — slow opacity waves across the ASCII grid
Cursor halo — characters near cursor climb the ramp
Border beams — warm pixel traces section dividers
SCROLL-TRIGGERED (once per section)
Density erosion — field parts to reveal content
Header decode — section titles scramble through ramp
Text generate — narrative text appears word-by-word
Entry stagger — work cards reveal sequentially
HOVER/CLICK (user-driven)
Field bloom — ASCII brightens around hovered content
ScrambleLink — CTA text scrambles on hover
Magnetic CTA — email link drifts toward cursor
Card expand — work entries expand on click
SCROLL-LINKED (continuous)
Breathing tempo — field pulse speed shifts per section
Footer gravity — field densifies at page bottom
Hero dissolve — wordmark fades on scroll (built)
